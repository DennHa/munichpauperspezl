📅 Tournament Schedule – 25. April 2025

  • Registration: Online via Deck & Dice Website
  • Location: 📍 Deck & Dice, Grillparzerstraße 14, 81675 München
  • Player Meeting: 25. April, starts at 11:30 – please arrive on time!
  • Rounds Begin: First round kicks off at 12:00 sharp.
  • End Time: Expected to wrap up between 18:00–19:00 depending on attendance.

🏗️ Tournament Structure

  • Format: Pauper (only commons, according to official legality).
  • Structure: Swiss rounds only — no Top 8 playoffs (sorry! 😢). Total rounds based on player count.
  • Round Timing: 50-minute rounds, best-of-three matches.

📜 Deck Rules

  • All cards must be Pauper-legal. Check the current ban list.
  • Decklists must be submitted the evening before the event. Way of submission will be announced in advance.

🤝 Conduct & Fair Play

  • REL: Regular Rules Enforcement Level — focused on fun, learning, and community play. 💬
  • Judges: On-site to help with rules questions or disputes. Their word is final.
  • Vibes: Be kind, be cool. Respect your opponents, judges, and the game. 🙌

🏆 Prizes

  • 🥇 Prizes based on standings — players choose from a prize pool after the event!

Servus, We Are The Pauper Spezl 🃏🍻

Pauper Spezl is a group of players who share a passion for the most common Magic: The Gathering™ format: Pauper. We meet regularly at a cozy Wirtshaus in Munich to play everything from wild brews to the most competitive meta decks 🍻

Whether you’re a spike chasing victory 🏆, just here to enjoy your 8-Snails deck 🐌🐌 or if you are just interested to play Magic with some nice people, you’ll find a warm welcome with us. Everyone is invited to join the fun, no matter your play style!

✨ So, come by and become a Pauper Spezl today! ✨

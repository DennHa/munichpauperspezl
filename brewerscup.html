Brewer's Cup

Pauper Brewing Competition

What is the Brewer's Cup?

The Brewer's Cup is our special event series dedicated to the creative side of Pauper Magic. Forget the meta — this is where original deck builders shine! Bring your wildest brews, your spiciest tech, and your most creative commons to compete for brewing glory.

How It Works

  • Format: Pauper with a twist — creativity and originality are rewarded
  • Judging: Decks may be evaluated on originality, theme, and competitive viability
  • Prizes: Bragging rights, trophies, and special prizes for the most creative builds

Why Brew?

  • Show off your deck-building skills beyond netdecking
  • Discover cards and strategies you never knew existed
  • Inspire others to think outside the box
  • Celebrate what makes Pauper great — accessible, creative Magic

Schedule & Rules

🃏 Schedule & Rules

Our Pauper event is designed to strike a perfect balance between competitive gameplay ⚔️ and a fun, casual atmosphere. Whether you're here to win or just enjoy the Pauper format, we’re excited to have you join us! 😊

If you have any questions, need to borrow a deck for the tournament, or just want to see if we’re nice people, come visit us at
Pauper Spezl Discord

📅 Tournament Schedule

  • Registration: Online via xxx
  • Location: 📍 Deck & Dice Munich, Grillparzerstraße 14, 81675 München
  • Player Meeting: 08. February, Starts at 11:30. Please be on time to avoid delays.
  • Rounds Begin: First round starts promptly at 12:00.
  • Expected End Time: The event is expected to conclude by 18:00 and 19:00, depending on the number of players and rounds.

🏗️ Tournament Structure

  • Format: Pauper (deck construction must adhere to Pauper legality).
  • Structure: Swiss rounds only (sorry, no time for Top 8 😢). The number of Swiss rounds will depend on attendance.
  • Round Timing: Each round is 50 minutes, with a best-of-three games format.

📜 Deck Rules

  • All cards must be legal in the Pauper format according to the most recent ban list.
  • Decklists must be submitted before the tournament starts. Please bring a clearly written or better yet, a printed decklist. We appreciate if you add a link to Moxfield or Archidekt to your decklist.

🤝 Conduct and Fair Play

  • Rules Enforcement Level (REL): This event follows Regular REL guidelines. Regular tournaments are focused on fun and social aspects, not enforcement.
  • Judges: Judges will be available during the event to assist with rulings and disputes. Their decisions are final.
  • Sportsmanship: We encourage a friendly and inclusive atmosphere. Treat your opponents and judges with respect. 🙌

🏆 Prizes

  • 🥇 Prizes will be awarded based on standings as pick and choose after the event.

Servus, We Are The Pauper Spezl 🃏🍻

Pauper Spezl is a group of players who share a passion for the most common Magic: The Gathering™ format: Pauper. We meet regularly at a cozy Wirtshaus in Munich to play everything from wild brews to the most competitive meta decks 🍻

Whether you’re a spike chasing victory 🏆, just here to enjoy your 8-Snails deck 🐌🐌 or if you are just interested to play Magic with some nice people, you’ll find a warm welcome with us. Everyone is invited to join the fun, no matter your play style!

✨ So, come by and become a Pauper Spezl today! ✨

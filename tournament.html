Pauper Spezl Tournament: Schedule & Rules

Our Pauper Spezl #3 event is all about exciting, skillful Magic in a welcoming, community-driven setting. Whether you're chasing the win or just want to jam some spicy commons, you're in the right place!

Tournament Schedule – 25. April 2026

  • Registration: Online via Deck & Dice Website
  • Location: Deck & Dice, Grillparzerstraße 14, 81675 München
  • Player Meeting: 25. April, starts at 11:30
  • Rounds Begin: First round kicks off at 12:00 sharp
  • End Time: Expected to wrap up between 18:00–19:00

Tournament Structure

  • Format: Pauper (only commons, according to official legality)
  • Structure: Swiss rounds only — no Top 8 playoffs
  • Round Timing: 50-minute rounds, best-of-three matches

Conduct & Fair Play

  • REL: Regular Rules Enforcement Level — focused on fun, learning, and community play
  • Judges: On-site to help with rules questions or disputes
  • Vibes: Be kind, be cool. Respect your opponents, judges, and the game

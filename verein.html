Pauper Spezl e.V.

Our Verein in Gründung

Servus, We Are Pauper Spezl

Pauper Spezl is a group of players who share a passion for the most common Magic: The Gathering™ format: Pauper. We meet regularly at a cozy Wirtshaus in Munich to play everything from wild brews to the most competitive meta decks.

Whether you're a spike chasing victory, just here to enjoy your 8-Snails deck or if you are just interested to play Magic with some nice people, you'll find a warm welcome with us. Everyone is invited to join the fun, no matter your play style!

Becoming a Verein

We are currently in the process of founding Pauper Spezl e.V. — a registered association (eingetragener Verein) under German law. This step allows us to build a sustainable structure for our community, organize events more effectively, and grow Pauper in the Munich area and beyond.

What Does This Mean?

  • Official Structure: A proper legal framework for organizing tournaments, meetups, and community events
  • Community-Driven: Members will have a voice in shaping the direction of Pauper Spezl
  • Sustainability: A Verein provides long-term stability for our growing community

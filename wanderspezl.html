Wanderspezl & Brewer's Cup

Monthly Pauper Tournaments & Brewing Competitions

What is the Wanderspezl?

The Wanderspezl is our monthly Pauper tournament series that travels to different locations across Munich and the surrounding area. Each month, a different local game store or venue hosts the event — giving you a chance to explore new spots and meet fellow Pauper players from across the region.

How It Works

  • Frequency: Once a month
  • Entry: Check our Discord for the latest event details and sign-up info
  • Location List: View all Wanderspezl locations

Upcoming Events

Stay up to date on the next Wanderspezl event — join our Discord for announcements, location details, and sign-ups!

Brewer's Cup

The Brewer's Cup is our special event series dedicated to the creative side of Pauper Magic. Forget the meta — this is where original deck builders shine! Bring your wildest brews, your spiciest tech, and your most creative commons to compete for brewing glory.

How It Works

  • Format: Pauper with a twist — creativity and originality are rewarded
  • Ban List: View the Brewer's Cup ban list

Want to enter? Keep an eye on our Discord for upcoming Brewer's Cup events, themes, and rules!

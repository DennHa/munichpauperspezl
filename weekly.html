Weekly Pauper Meetups

Pauper Spezl organizes regular weekly meetups across multiple locations in Bavaria. Whether you're in Munich, Landsberg, Augsburg, or Rosenheim — there's a table waiting for you!

Where & When

Our weekly meetups happen at local game stores and venues. Check our Discord for the most up-to-date schedule, as times and locations can change.

Locations

  • München
  • Landsberg
  • Augsburg
  • Rosenheim

What to Expect

  • Casual and competitive Pauper games
  • A welcoming atmosphere for new and experienced players
  • No registration needed — just show up (there are plenty Pauper decks to test)
  • Great people, good vibes, and sometimes cold beers

Find Your Local Group

Join our Discord to find the meetup closest to you and stay updated on any schedule changes!
